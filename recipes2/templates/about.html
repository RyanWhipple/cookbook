About

CookBook is a portfolio project by Wesley Giles and Ryan Whipple It combines recipe book features with a social media feed. Users can:

  • Post their own recipes
  • Post the Results from cooking recipes, both their own and those of other users
  • Follow other users and see their new Recipes and Results in the Social Feed

CookBook is built in Python using Flask with a MySQL database.
